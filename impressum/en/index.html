Content & Streams

WaveLoka provides access to publicly available internet radio streams operated by independent, third-party broadcasting stations. WaveLoka does not operate, host, transmit, modify, or redistribute any audio content, including music, speech, news, or any other material. WaveLoka functions solely as a directory and technical interface to these publicly reachable streams.

Ads Injected by Radio Stations

Some radio stations insert their own advertisements directly into their audio stream. WaveLoka has no control over these ads. They are not placed, selected, or influenced by WaveLoka and are not subject to the Ad-Free feature.

WaveLoka Ads & Ad-Free Feature

WaveLoka displays its own advertisements: a banner at the bottom of the screen and a short audio pre-roll played once per session and after every three station switches.

The Ad-Free feature removes only these WaveLoka-served advertisements for the remainder of the day. It does not affect advertisements embedded in radio streams by the stations themselves.

Liability

Radio streams accessible through WaveLoka are provided by their respective broadcasting stations. WaveLoka only provides a technical interface to these publicly available streams and has no influence over their operation. Availability, content, and licensing of each stream are the sole responsibility of the broadcasting station providing it. WaveLoka accepts no liability for stream content.

Notice for Radio Broadcasters

WaveLoka’s station catalog is manually curated. WaveLoka works exclusively with links: stream addresses and logos are referenced by URL and loaded at runtime — no stream or image files are stored within the app.

Trademarks

Station names and logos displayed in WaveLoka are trademarks of their respective owners and are used solely to identify the linked radio streams. No affiliation with, sponsorship by, or endorsement from the respective broadcasters is implied.

Station Inclusion (Opt-In)

If you would like your station added to WaveLoka, or want to provide your official logo, please email us the following details:

📧 optin@waveloka.app

• Station name
• Official stream URL (link)
• Station website
• Logo as a publicly reachable HTTPS image link

For best display quality in Android Auto, the logo should be square (1:1), in PNG or JPG format, at least 512 × 512 pixels, and served over HTTPS.

Station Removal (Opt-Out)

If you would like your station removed from WaveLoka, please email us the station name and stream URL:

📧 optout@waveloka.app
The station will be removed within 48 hours of a verified request.
