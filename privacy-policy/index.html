2. What Data We Collect

WaveLoka is designed to collect as little data as possible. Here is what the app processes:

  • Location data — Your location is used to show radio stations near you; depending on availability, this may include approximate or precise location. The parking feature (Find My Car) additionally uses precise location — see Section 3. Processing happens on your device; location data is not stored on our servers and is not shared with third parties.
  • Favorites and listening history — Stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data.
  • Advertising identifiers — Used by Google AdMob to serve ads. See Section 4.

3. Parking feature (Find My Car)

When you use the "Find My Car" feature, the app stores your vehicle's position (coordinates, time, accuracy). This happens automatically when the Android Auto connection is disconnected, or manually when you choose to. This data is stored exclusively on your device, is never transmitted to us or third parties, and can be deleted in the app at any time. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (provision of the feature).

4. Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account registration, no email, no name required
  • No location data transmitted to our servers (see Sections 2 and 3)
  • No listening behavior sent to our servers
  • No tracking across apps or websites by WaveLoka itself

5. Advertising (Google AdMob)

WaveLoka uses Google AdMob to display banner ads and audio pre-roll ads. AdMob may collect and use:

  • Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID / AAID)
  • Approximate location
  • Device information

This data is processed by Google LLC under their own privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

In the EEA, AdMob only personalises ads with your consent, which is requested on first launch via a consent dialog (UMP SDK).

Ad-Free feature: The Ad-Free feature removes only ads served by WaveLoka — the banner at the bottom of the screen and the audio pre-roll (played once per session and after every three station switches). It does not affect advertisements that radio stations insert directly into their own streams. WaveLoka has no control over those stream-injected ads.

6. Radio Streams & Third-Party Data Collection

WaveLoka does not host, provide, or relay any audio streams of its own. Every stream is published and operated by the respective radio broadcaster. When you play a station, WaveLoka connects your device directly to the audio stream server operated by that broadcaster. As part of this connection, the station's server receives your IP address and may collect other technical data such as connection time, data volume, and streaming client information — as part of normal server operation.

WaveLoka has no control over, and bears no responsibility for, any data collected by radio stations or their streaming infrastructure. Each station operates under its own privacy policy. If you have concerns about a specific station's data practices, please refer to that station's privacy policy directly.

This data flow is inherent to how internet radio streaming works and cannot be prevented while using the app's core playback functionality.

7. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Location — Legitimate interest (showing relevant local content), Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. You can disable location permission at any time in Android system settings.
  • Advertising identifiers — Your consent, collected via the consent dialog on first launch, Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR. You may withdraw consent at any time in the app's Ad Preferences settings.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, and restrict processing of your personal data, as well as the right to object and data portability.

Since WaveLoka does not store personal data on its own servers, most of these rights are exercised through your Android device settings or Google's privacy tools at myaccount.google.com.

For any privacy requests: contact@waveloka.app

9. Data Retention

WaveLoka stores no personal data on its own servers. All local data (favorites, history, preferences) remains on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

10. Children

WaveLoka is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
